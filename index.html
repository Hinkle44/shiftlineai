Sound Familiar?

Every shop has the same four problems.

They cost you time, money, and customers. Most owners just accept them as part of running a shop.

⏳

Jobs that sit without being started

Urgent work gets buried. No one knows what to run first. Someone has to walk the floor and figure it out every single morning, and things still get missed.

📞

Customers calling for status updates

Where are my parts? When will they ship? These calls pull someone off the floor and signal to customers that your operation is reactive, not on top of it.

📋

Manual quoting that eats your afternoon

Specs come in by email. You calculate by hand. Details get missed. The quote goes out late, and by then the customer has already called someone else.

🔴

Urgent labels that get ignored

The sticker works until it doesn't. On a busy floor, physical flags get overlooked. One missed due date and you're eating a rush fee or losing an account you worked to get.
